Welcome to SPARCL's documentation!
==================================
SPectra Analysis & Retrievable Catalog Lab (SPARCL) created by the Spectro Project Team at NOIRLab’s Astro Data Lab consists of services, tools, and example workflows centered around optical and near-infrared spectroscopy. The SPARCL service currently deployed is focused on serving SDSS-DR16 and DESI spectra, and in particular features a novel spectral data access, reaching 100x faster retrieval speeds compared to other services and tools. This service will take advantage of the existing data discovery from database tables stored at the Astro Data Lab, and retrieve spectra from SDSS and DESI in formats ready to use for spectral analysis using standard and astronomy Python software libraries.
